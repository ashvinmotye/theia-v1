Introducing Theia

First launched in 2016, Theia has received a warm welcome from our targeted users as well as general public. Theia has taken the market by storm and is continuing to impress users on a daily basis.

Reviewers and users all alike have come under the mesmerising simplicity of the application, seduced by the overall UI and UX. The smoothness and accuracy of the app have been the most appreciated features.

Theia has been developed by technology enthusiast and student at the University of Mauritius, Akshi Boojhawon, in her final year project and co-engineered by Ashvin Motye.
